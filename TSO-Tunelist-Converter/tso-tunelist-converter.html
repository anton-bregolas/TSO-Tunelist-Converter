Paste a link to your Tunebook or list of Tagged tunes into the input form below, press the big button and wait a wee bit.

NB: only TheSession.org links ending with /tunes or /tunebook, as well as /sets and /tunesets are accepted. You may need to add /tunes or /tunesets at the end of your URL.

When the Tunetable is ready, press Sort button once to open sorting menu, select sorting style and press the button again. Use the Download button to save your Tunetable in .txt format.
