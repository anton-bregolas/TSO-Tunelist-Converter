How to sort tunes

When the Tunetable is ready, press the Sort button once to open sorting menu, select the preferred sorting style and press the button again to confirm. Your tunes / sets can either be left in the default TSO order or be sorted anew in alphabetical order. Depending on the options picked, Sort will also (re)move the articles in tune titles to make skimming through the list easier.

+
How to save tunes

Use the Download button to save your Tunetable in plain text (.txt) format. If you want to save the data as .json, press the Index (#) button on the Tunetable.

Should you wish to revert your tune titles to TSO defaults before saving the Tunetable, press the Revert button to the right of Tunetable Name.
